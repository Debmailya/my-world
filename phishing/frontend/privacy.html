Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Introduction

PhishGuard AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 URLs You Submit

When you submit a URL for analysis, we process that URL to detect phishing threats. However:

  • URLs are NOT stored in our database
  • URLs are NOT logged for analytics
  • URLs are NOT shared with third parties
  • URLs are only held in memory during processing

1.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP address (for rate limiting and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time spent

1.3 Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Session management
  • Security purposes
  • User preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide phishing detection services
  • Prevent abuse and fraud
  • Improve our detection algorithms
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Secure data centers with physical security
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Encryption at rest for any stored data

4. Data Retention

We retain data as minimally as possible:

  • URLs are not retained after analysis
  • Access logs are retained for 30 days
  • Anonymous usage statistics are retained indefinitely

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Hosting and infrastructure (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics (privacy-focused tools only)
  • Email service provider

These providers have their own privacy policies. We ensure they meet our privacy standards.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Data portability

Contact us at privacy@phishguard.ai to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

PhishGuard AI does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our website.
